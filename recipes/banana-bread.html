Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a moist, sweet loaf made with ripe bananas. It's a great way to use up overripe bananas and is perfect for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • baking spray with flour
  • 1 1/4 cups mashed riped bananas
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups old fashioned rolled oats

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan with baking spray.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mashed bananas, honey, baking soda, and salt until well combined.
  3. Add milk, oil, egg, vanilla extract, almond extract, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Mix until smooth.
  4. In another bowl, combine flour and baking powder. Gradually add to the wet mixture.
  5. Fold in rolled oats until evenly distributed.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let it cool before slicing. Enjoy!
